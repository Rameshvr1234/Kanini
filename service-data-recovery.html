End-to-End Data Recovery

From accidental deletion to damaged drives, Kanini Technology recovers data from HDDs, SSDs and external storage. Our lab handles logical corruption, firmware failures and chip-level recovery with strict confidentiality.

Every job starts with a non-intrusive diagnosis. We image failing drives with specialised hardware, safeguard the original media and deliver verified data sets. NDA-based projects and business SLAs available.

We support consumer drives, enterprise RAID disks, USB media and memory cards. Clean-room partners are engaged for head-stack swaps when required, ensuring an end-to-end solution.

We Recover

  • Formatted or deleted partitions
  • Non-detecting or clicking drives
  • SSD firmware/controller failures
  • Encrypted drives with valid keys
  • USB / SD / CF flash media
  • NAS & RAID member disks

Delivery & Assurance

  • Encrypted handover media on request
  • Integrity check report with recovered data
  • Standby storage devices available for purchase
  • Secure data wiping of failed drives (optional)
  • Confidential handling backed by NDAs
  • Free pickup within 5km of Coimbatore
